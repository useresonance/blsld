About ITpedia

ITpedia consists of a database with articles, analyzes, etc. about IT. Companies, persons and organizations can submit these articles and share them with each other according to the Wiki principle. The goal is that IT people help each other and provide solutions to problems. New products and solutions can also be made public through ITpedia. An IT IT and review tool is included in ITpedia. With this IT tool, the quality of automation projects, information systems, organizations and implementations can be assessed using checklists.
